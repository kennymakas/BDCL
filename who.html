Features of an Architectural Firm’s Website Have?

For your website to be successful, there are things unique to the architectural design industry that need to be incorporated into your firm’s website, such as:

  1. Website content that provides a clear description of your business and types of projects your firm offers
  2. A domain name that matches your firm’s registered name (if at all possible).
  3. Visible and quickly found contact information on your home page, landing pages, header, and footer.
  4. Client testimonials on your home page (if you’ve already completed some projects.)
  5. User-friendly and intuitive website design that is easy to navigate.
  6. Correctly sized and SEO optimized images showcasing your architectural projects.
  7. Optimize your architect website for search engines with SEO for architects.
